Goa Gajah

The Goa Gajah tourist attraction is actually a historical relic, as the name suggests is in the form of a cave in a temple area, so it is known as Goa Gajah Temple. The location is in the village of Bedulu, Blahbatuh District, Gianyar Regency. Its location is close to Ubud tourism so it is better known as the Goa Gajah Ubud tourist attraction.

The Goa Gajah tourist complex also has a temple building which is estimated to have been built in the XI century AD during the reign of the Warmadewa royal dynasty, the temple functioned as a holy place for Buddhists and Hindus, this ancient Balinese cultural heritage indicates that this cave and temple came from relics of ancient Balinese kingdoms.

Terasering Tegalalang Ubud

Before the tourism industry in Ubud grew as fast as it is today, most local residents in Ubud made their living as farmers, specializing in rice farming.

Although nowadays most of the local residents in Ubud are changing their way of earning a living by engaging in the tourism industry. Some of them remain rice farmers. This is why many rice fields are maintained in Ubud Bali. Not only the usual form of rice fields, in Ubud you will see many terraced rice fields. One of the most beautiful terraced rice fields in Ubud is located in Tegalalang Village, Ubud.

The Tegalalang rice terraces have now become a famous tourist attraction in Bali. In addition, many tourists who previously vacationed in Bali recommend the Tegalalang terraced rice fields as a must-visit vacation spot in Bali.

Air Terjun Tegenungan

The tourist attraction of the Tegenungan Gianyar waterfall also has another name, namely Kemenuh waterfall. Because the waterfall is located in the village of Kemenuh, Gianyar district. The address or location of the Kemenuh waterfall is in Kemenuh Village which is the Sukawati District, Gianyar Regency.

When you arrive at the vehicle parking location, you will see many small shops selling souvenirs for souvenirs. You will also see simple stalls selling food and drinks. Fried noodles, snacks and young coconut are available. From the vehicle parking area, to reach the location of the waterfall, you have to walk down the stairs.
